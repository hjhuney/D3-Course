SEC Quarterbacks by INT-Modified Yards per Attempt

Stat Descriptions

YPA: Yards per pass attempt

YPC: Yards per pass completion

Long: Longest completed pass by QB/p>

TDs: Touchdown passes

INTs: Interceptions thrown by QB

SacksTaken: Total times QB was sacked

QBRating: Quarterback rating

AttemptsPerINT: Ratio of pass attempts to interceptions thrown

INTModYards: Total yards modified by -50 for every interception thrown

INTModYardsPerAtt: This takes the "INTModYards" metric and then takes average modified yards per pass attempt

AttemptsPerSack: Ratio of pass attempts (plus sacks) to number of times sacked
